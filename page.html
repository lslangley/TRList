Transit agencies providing Royale to their riders

The following agencies provide free Royale subscriptions to their frequent riders. To learn more about Royale and the features it includes, please consult our help page linked here.

If your transit agency does not provide free Royale subscriptions, learn more about how you can subscribe to Royale by purchasing it directly in the app.
